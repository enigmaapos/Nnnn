NFTs and Their Relationship to Digital Assets

  • Digital assets include images, videos, music, software, etc.
  • NFTs bring ownership and scarcity to these assets.
  • Think of NFTs as certificates of authenticity.
  • Without NFTs, files can be copied. With NFTs, ownership is provable and secure.

Conclusion: NFTs bring value, uniqueness, and traceability to digital assets.

The Purpose of an NFT Marketplace

  • A place where creators, buyers, and sellers meet.
  • Examples: OpenSea, Blur, Magic Eden, Coreverse Marketplace, PiNFT
  • Functions:
    • Mint (create) NFTs
    • List & sell NFTs
    • Buy & trade NFTs
    • View ownership, royalties, and stats

Purpose: Create a liquid, accessible, and valuable digital asset economy.
