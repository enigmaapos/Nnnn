NFTs and Their Relationship to Digital Assets

  • Digital assets include anything stored digitally: images, videos, music, software, etc.
  • NFTs represent ownership and scarcity of these assets.
  • Think of NFTs as the certificate of authenticity and title deed for digital content.
  • Without NFTs, digital files can be copied infinitely. With NFTs, ownership is provable and secure.

Conclusion: NFTs bring value, uniqueness, and traceability to digital assets.

The Purpose of an NFT Marketplace

  • A platform where creators, buyers, and sellers meet.
  • Examples: OpenSea, Blur, Magic Eden, Coreverse Marketplace (custom), PiNFT, etc.
  • Provides tools to:
    • Mint (create) NFTs
    • List & sell NFTs
    • Buy & trade NFTs
    • View ownership history, royalties, and collection stats

Purpose: NFT marketplaces create an economy for digital assets, making them liquid, accessible, and valuable.

How to Earn Using NFTs

  • Create & Sell NFTs (Artist/Creator)

    Upload art, music, photos, or 3D models. Mint your creation as an NFT. List it on a marketplace. Earn income from the initial sale and royalties from resales.

  • Trade NFTs (Investor/Collector)

    Buy low, sell high. Find rare or trending NFTs and flip them for a profit. Similar to trading stocks or crypto, but based on scarcity and demand.

  • Earn Royalties

    Set a royalty percentage during minting. Every time the NFT resells, you earn a passive income.

  • Play-to-Earn Games

    Use NFT characters or items in blockchain games. Win rewards that can be sold or traded for crypto.

  • Stake NFTs

    Some platforms allow staking NFTs to earn tokens or rewards.

  • Use NFTs for Access or Membership

    Exclusive NFTs can be sold that give VIP access, early drops, or premium content.
