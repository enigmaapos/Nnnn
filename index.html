NFTs and Their Relationship to Digital Assets

  • Digital assets include anything stored digitally: images, videos, music, software, etc.
  • NFTs represent ownership and scarcity of these assets.
  • Think of NFTs as the certificate of authenticity and title deed for digital content.
  • Without NFTs, digital files can be copied infinitely. With NFTs, ownership is provable and secure.

Conclusion: NFTs bring value, uniqueness, and traceability to digital assets.
